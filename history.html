History

University Boxing was started in 2007 to create a community where individuals could meet up and and practice the sport of boxing. No other boxing organization existed in the University of Texas. University Boxing was designed to be a community of learning, and people of all skill levels were to be accepted.

It started with a few black-and-white flyers created on Microsoft Paint, tacked on to wooden kiosks around campus. The first meeting had about 5 members who had never known each other before.

The organization slowly grew by word of mouth over the years. Though it had humble beginnings, University Boxing has persisted through the years. It is now a thriving environment for the UT student community, with members helping each other and growing together.

Hundreds of people have gone through the organization over the years. More than forty active members now enroll in the club each semester.
