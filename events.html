Since its inception, University Boxing has been branching out its network for the purpose of achieving its goals of spreading boxing throughout the state of Texas. We participate in events like the annual Sigma Chi charity Fight Night and Forty Acres Fest.

We have social events throughout the year. Some of us are talented cooks and we usually have barbeque get-togethers when the weather's good. Or else we go get some wings at Pluckers when we're too lazy. All that working out takes a lot of energy, so we love to eat!
