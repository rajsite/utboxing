Meetings

Practice typically consists of circuits that move in timed rounds. Members always work and move in pairs. The circuits include heavybag training, speedbag, shadow-boxing with weights, mitt-work, and jump rope. Everything revolves around the clock. Timing is very important to us, much like it is in a real boxing bout. After the circuits, there are group exercises for the conditioning and fitness that is required in boxers. All workouts end with floor work, where members circle up and are led in a series of core-building exercises.
