Reagan High School Program

Starting Spring 2011, University Boxing has teamed up with the administration at Reagan High School in North Austin to establish a boxing/mentoring program for students of Reagan High.

Members of the organization volunteer on a rotating schedule to help coach the students in an after-school program. We have coordinated with the high school on purchasing equipment. Much of their training facilities have been structured to mirror our own, so that similar workouts can be created for their students.

Meetings are scheduled at Reagan High School for 1 hour practices. Monday, Wednesday 5-6pm.
