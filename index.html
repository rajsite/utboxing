University Boxing Home

University Boxing is a non-profit boxing student organization at the University of Texas at Austin. It was established to bring in the sport and art of the sweet science to the University of Texas student body.

Members meet and engage in the disciplines and techniques of the sport, training and learning together. Workouts are structured around boxing-specific drills, with periodical instruction interspersed in between. Our training area includes a heavy bag, speed bags, and ample open space for mitt work, jump-rope, and other boxing related exercises.

We believe in giving back to the community, and we often involve ourselves in service projects. We have started a program with Reagan High School to mentor and teach boxing to students. For more information, please click on Reagan High.

University Boxing is open to all current students at the Universty of Texas. No prior experience in boxing or any martial art is required. Join us on Facebook!
